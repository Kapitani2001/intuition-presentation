Premise 1

Innocence Is the Bridge

The first step to superconscious awareness

"We go into innocence by letting go of our need to know or our need to understand... allowing ourselves to be empty and not know."

An empty, receptive state

Not a childish state — it is where you stop gripping "knowing." The child in innocence is an emptiness, a receptive state of mind.

Innocence is an agendaless state

Always go back to the inner child... the creative child. Because innocence is the doorway through which everything flows.

Premise 2

Stop "Making Sense"

What blocks it

Editing what comes through because it feels unfamiliar or doesn't fit your story

Needing everything to be logical and explainable

The discipline

Let it come through as it is

Intuition speaks a different language — honor that

"Needing everything to make sense
keeps you confined to the known."

Letting go of that opens creative intelligence

Premise 3

Own What You Get

💡

Something occurs to you

An impression, a symbol, a knowing

➔

✋

Hold it. Claim it.

Even if it feels impossible or uncomfortable

The training is to hold what you receive

Another part of you will try to reject it as impossible, unreasonable, or uncomfortable.

That rejection is the conscious mind trying to maintain control.
Let the intuition stand.

Premise 4

Agenda Corrupts Intuition

Innocence is an agendaless state

When there is something at stake — when you need a specific answer — intuition disappears. The faculty requires that you have nothing riding on the outcome.

Dysfunctional applications

Using intuition to satisfy low-level agendas corrupts the faculty:

"Does he love me?" Stock market predictions Seeking reassurance Control & certainty

"When something is at stake,
intuition disappears."

Premise 5

No Fixing. No Coaching.

Be a clean channel

In readings or practice, the discipline is to deliver what is obvious and what occurs to you — without wrapping it in advice, solutions, or rescue.

✕ Not this

"You should try..."

"What if you..."

"The solution is..."

Rescuer mode = agenda

✓ This

"What I notice is..."

"What occurs to me is..."

"What jumps out is..."

Clean channel = no agenda

Getting Into Innocence

The protocol

1

Let go of the need to know

"Allow yourself to be empty and not know."

Release the need to understand. Surrender the grip on certainty. Needing to know is a tension — recognize it, hang out with it, and just let go.

2

Observe the obvious

Observe what jumps out — what is obvious — without forcing interpretation.

Don't analyze. Notice. Go with the first thing you get. The first impulse is the cleanest — the second is the editor.

3

Make it up

Unpack with imagination — without pretending you're causing it.

Imagination is the "psychic generator." Always assume there's more. Be specific.

Innocence → Observe the obvious → Make it up

The Paradox to Teach

The harder you try to be intuitive,
the less intuitive you become.

Effort belongs to the conscious mind.

Intuition lives in effortlessness.

The instruction isn't "try harder"

— it's "get out of the way."

That's what innocence really is:
getting yourself out of the way.
